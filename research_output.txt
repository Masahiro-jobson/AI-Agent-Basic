 The Ussuri brown bear (Ursus arctos lasiotus), also known as the Ezo bear, is a subspecies of brown bear native to Northeast Asia. It is one of the largest brown bears, and it inhabits the forested areas of the Russian Far East, Northeast China, and Hokkaido, Japan. The Ezo bear is known for its thick fur, which helps it survive in the cold climates of its range.
